Problem-Solving Case Study — Best Krazy Chicken (BKC)

For this task, I developed a restaurant concept called Best Krazy Chicken (BKC), designed to compete with popular UK chains like Nando’s. The idea introduces a Colombian-style wood-fired rotisserie chicken with authentic flavours and traditional side dishes. The focus is not only on food quality but also on building a strong digital presence.

Context

BKC serves chicken marinated with Colombian spices and roasted slowly over firewood, giving it a distinctive flavour. Customers can enjoy side dishes such as wood-roasted potatoes, plantain, cassava, and homemade sauces like ají and chimichurri. This concept fills a gap in the UK food market by offering flavours not yet available locally.

Challenges & Opportunities

  • Challenge: Competing with established restaurant chains in the UK.
  • Challenge: Building brand recognition from scratch.
  • Opportunity: Offering unique Colombian flavours with no current competitors in this niche.
  • Opportunity: Using digital platforms to reach and engage customers effectively.

Design Choices

The page uses a clear, image-led hero to communicate the product and brand quickly. Social links and contact details are easy to find to reduce friction for engagement. A simple contact form enables quick enquiries (mailto-based, no database needed). Color accents follow the BKC logo (red with blue outline) for consistent branding across the site.
