Problem-Solving Case Study — Crazzy Chicken Restaurant

For this task, I designed a digital presence for a restaurant concept called Crazzy Chicken Restaurant, a business idea aimed at competing with popular chains like Nando’s in the UK. The goal was to analyze its challenges, highlight growth opportunities, and demonstrate how technology can improve customer reach and service quality.

Context

Crazzy Chicken Restaurant offers high-quality grilled chicken with a unique flavor and friendly service. While the food and concept are strong, the restaurant needs a solid online presence to compete with established brands. My solution focused on creating a simple website with clear navigation, links to social media, and a direct contact form.
